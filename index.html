Sahyog Foundation exists to offer sustained support to underserved communities across India. We focus on three pillars:

  • Education: Scholarships, after-school centres, teacher training.
  • Health: Preventative camps, maternal health awareness, sanitation drives.
  • Livelihoods: Vocational training, micro-enterprise support, digital literacy.

We partner with local NGOs, panchayats, and volunteers to keep projects community-led and sustainable.
